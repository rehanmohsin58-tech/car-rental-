Our Story

Founded in 2010, Rehan Car Rental began with a simple vision: to make luxury car rental accessible to everyone. What started as a small fleet of just 5 vehicles has grown into one of the most trusted names in the industry.

Today, we operate a fleet of over 500 premium vehicles, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the region. Our commitment to quality, transparency, and exceptional customer service remains at the heart of everything we do.
